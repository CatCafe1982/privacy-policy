<p><em>Last Updated: November 12, 2025</em></p>


<h2>1. Types of Data We Collect</h2>


<h3>1.1 Personal Identifiable Information (PII)</h3>

<p>To provide account login, subscription verification, and personalized features, this App uses <strong>Sign in with Apple</strong> and collects:</p>


<ul>

  <li>uid</li>

  <li>email</li>

  <li>displayName</li>

  <li>isSubscribed</li>

  <li>subscriptionExpiryDate</li>

</ul>


<p>Apple handles authentication securely. No password is accessible to the developer.</p>


<h3>1.2 User-Generated Content</h3>

<p>User-created records include: <code>partNumber</code>, <code>workOrder</code>, <code>lotNumber</code>, <code>remark</code>, <code>station</code>, <code>machinesString</code>.</p>


<p>Stored in Firestore:</p>

<ul>

  <li>/groups/{groupId}/records/{recordId}</li>

  <li>/globalRecords/{recordId}</li>

</ul>


<h2>2. Purposes of Data Usage</h2>

<ul>

  <li>Account creation and maintenance</li>

  <li>Subscription verification</li>

  <li>Group collaboration support</li>

  <li>Technical support and product improvement</li>

</ul>


<h2>3. Data Storage and Processing</h2>

<p>Data stored in <strong>Firebase Firestore</strong> (Google LLC). Authentication via <strong>Sign in with Apple</strong> and <strong>StoreKit</strong>.</p>


<h2>4. Data Sharing</h2>


<h3>4.1 Sharing Between Users</h3>

<p>Group owners may invite members via codes. Joined users gain access to group data.</p>


<h3>4.2 Third-Party Sharing</h3>

<p>Other than Apple and Firebase services, <strong>no personal data is sold, rented, or shared.</strong></p>


<h2>5. Data Deletion and Cache Management</h2>
