Privacy Policy – QingTsaiWip V2

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

1. Types of Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

To provide account login, subscription verification, and personalized features, the App uses Sign in with Apple and collects:

  • uid
  • email
  • displayName
  • isSubscribed
  • subscriptionExpiryDate

Apple manages authentication securely. The developer has no access to passwords.

1.2 User-Generated Content

Users may create production records containing partNumber, workOrder, lotNumber, remark, station, machinesString, and more.

These are stored in Firestore:

  • /groups/{groupId}/records/{recordId}
  • /globalRecords/{recordId}

2. Purposes of Data Usage

  • Account creation and maintenance
  • Subscription verification
  • Group collaboration support
  • Technical support and service improvements

3. Data Storage and Processing

Data is stored in Firebase Firestore (Google LLC). Authentication is handled via Sign in with Apple and StoreKit.

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Sharing Between Users

Group owners may share data with invited members. Members who join a group gain access to shared data based on group permissions.

4.2 Third-Party Sharing

Other than Apple and Firebase services, no personal data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

5. Data Deletion and Cache Management

  • Users may delete individual or all production records
  • Group owners may delete entire groups (irreversible)
  • Local Firebase cache can be cleared from settings

6. Data Retention

Data is stored until the user deletes it. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

7. Data Security

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication security
  • Firestore Security Rules
  • Restricted developer access

8. Your Rights

  • View and update your account information
  • Delete your account or content
  • Submit privacy-related inquiries
