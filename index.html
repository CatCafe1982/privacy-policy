Privacy Policy for QingTsaiWip V2

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

1. Types of Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

To provide account login, subscription verification, and personalized features, this App uses Sign in with Apple and collects:

  • uid
  • email
  • displayName
  • isSubscribed
  • subscriptionExpiryDate

Apple handles authentication securely. No password is accessible to the developer.

1.2 User-Generated Content

User-created records include: partNumber, workOrder, lotNumber, remark, station, machinesString.

Stored in Firestore:

  • /groups/{groupId}/records/{recordId}
  • /globalRecords/{recordId}

2. Purposes of Data Usage

  • Account creation and maintenance
  • Subscription verification
  • Group collaboration support
  • Technical support and product improvement

3. Data Storage and Processing

Data stored in Firebase Firestore (Google LLC). Authentication via Sign in with Apple and StoreKit.

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Sharing Between Users

Group owners may invite members via codes. Joined users gain access to group data.

4.2 Third-Party Sharing

Other than Apple and Firebase services, no personal data is sold, rented, or shared.

5. Data Deletion and Cache Management

  • Delete individual or all records
  • Delete groups (via recursive Cloud Function)
  • Clear local Firebase cache

6. Data Retention

Data stored until user deletion. Deletion requests completed within 30 days.

7. Data Security

  • HTTPS / TLS
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore Security Rules
  • Developer access restricted

8. Your Rights

  • View/update account info
  • Delete account and content
  • Submit privacy-related inquiries
